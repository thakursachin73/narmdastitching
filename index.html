Our Services
Lock stitch

Used to make clothing, such as pants, shirts, and dresses.

Blanket stitch

A combination of one single crochet and two double crochet stitches. This stitch is used for throws and afghans.

Zigzag stitch

Used for finishing raw edges, preventing fraying, and creating stretchable seams.

Back stitch

An advanced variation of the running stitch that's solid, durable, and flexible.

Slip stitch

A basic crochet stitch that sits right next to the stitch it is made into.

Chain stitch

Used for outlining, such as the outside of letters and shapes.
